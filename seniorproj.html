ECE 493 senior project

Check out this cool video I made about my KEMET award winning senior design project!

I talk more about this project in my resume ( here! ) but I helped spec out the parts, designed and ordered the demultiplexing circuit for motor control, fabricated the majority of the electronics seen in the video, and came up with the idea to use the guitar case in the first place.

I have to give a lot of credit to my team mates for knocking it out of the park with the fast fourier transform algorithms, and for pursuing design methodology that was simple but always exceeded our criteria. Thanks to them we won the KEMET Award, a $500 prize that goes to the project that is voted on by a variety of staff and alumni to be the best.
